About Me

I have spent all of my life living around the water or spending my time on/in it, and I wouldn't ask for it to be any other way.

I am an active member in the Florida Xi chapter of Phi Delta Theta. Being in a fraternity has brought me on a path to becoming the best version of myself, and taught me valuable lessons along the way.

Who am I?

Born in Florida, I've been fortunate enough to experience great weather year-round. I have raced sailboats, snowboarded down mountains, and volunteered for various organizations while in the Boy Scouts of America; however, reading and learning new skill sets such as coding are a daily passion of mine.
